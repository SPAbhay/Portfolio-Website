About Me

I'm an AI Engineer driven by a deep curiosity for building intelligent systems from the ground up. My journey started in product design, where I developed a passion for understanding user needs and crafting elegant solutions. This design-centric approach now informs my engineering philosophy: I build AI not just to be technically robust, but also to be intuitive, purposeful, and genuinely useful.

I thrive on the challenges found at the intersection of research and production. Whether it's architecting a complex multi-agent system or deploying a scalable service on AWS, my goal is always the same: to take an idea from a whiteboard sketch to a reliable, high-impact product.
